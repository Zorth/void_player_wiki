#location

The Void is the space between stories, the nowhere that you need to get somewhere. Just like you cannot have light without darkness, you cannot have existence without **The Void**. 

As The Void exists on its own timeline. You do not age in The Void nor can you get offspring.

---
# The Code Legal
- I. Crimes against The Void, it's enforcers, or the council and their judgement are met with exile, death, or erasure.
- II. Crimes against individuals are generally settled in the fighting ring when those are able, servants of The Void are protected under the council and their court.
- III. Criminals may be branded on the neck (or equivalent area), a second brand means exile or death.
- IV. You are free to hold the religion you desire; You may not advertise your religion publicly.
