#npc [[Sythian]]
Barthalemuw is a Tripkee located in the [[Drake Tower]].
He and several other Tripkee's watch over one of the [[Hearts of Zurzalith]]. His town is being threatened by a demon [[Demon Lady in Red]], who will kill them should they revealed where the heart is located.
